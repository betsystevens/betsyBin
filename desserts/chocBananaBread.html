Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • ½
    cup butter, softened
  • 2
    eggs
  • 1½
    cups sugar
  • 2 or 3
    bananas, chunked & mashed
  • ½
    cup sour cream
  • 1
    teaspoon vanilla
  • 2
    cups flour (250g)
  • 1
    teaspoon baking powder
  • ½
    teaspoon baking soda
  • ½
    teaspoon salt
  • 1 to 1½
    cups mini chocolate chips

Preparation

  • Step 1.
  • Preheat oven to 350°. Grease 1 large or 2 medium loaf pans.
  • Step 2.
  • Mix four, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
  • Step 3.
  • Cream butter and sugar together. Add eggs, mix. Add sour cream, vanilla and bananas, mix.
  • Step 4.
  • Add flour mixture to wet ingredients and mix. Add chocolate chips.
  • Step 5.
  • Bake one hour for two medium loaves. Bake longer for one large loaf.

notes from the chef...

Leave most of the bananas in chunks, don't mash all of them. This will aid in getting the center of the loaves done.

I double this recipe and bake 3 medium loaves. They are prefectly done in exactly one hour.
