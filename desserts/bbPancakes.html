Blueberry Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼
    cups flour (160g)
  • 1
    T baking powder
  • 1
    T sugar
  • 1
    teaspoon salt
  • 1
    cup milk
  • 1
    egg
  • 2
    T oil
  • 1
    pint blueberries
  • 1 ½
    bananas, diced

Preparation

  • Step 1.
  • Mix dry ingredients.
  • Step 2.
  • Mix milk, egg and oil.
  • Step 3.
  • Add liquid to dry ingredients. Mix lightly, leaving plenty of lumps.
  • Step 4.
  • Add blueberries & bananas. Let batter rest around 10 minutes.
  • Step 5.
  • Cook in non-stick pan over medium to low.

notes from the chef...

I reserve about ¼ cup of the fruit for the inevitable fruitless batter at the bottom of the bowl.
