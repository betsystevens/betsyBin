Cauliflower Salad

Ingredients

  • 1
    large head lettuce, cut up
  • 1
    red onion, chopped
  • 1
    lb bacon, fried & crumbled
  • 1
    head cauliflower, cut up
  • 2
    cups shredded mozzerella cheese
  • ⅔
    cups sugar
  • 2
    cups Miracle Whip
  • 2
    salt, pepper

Preparation

  • Step 1.
  • Layer ingredients through cheese in order listed.
  • Step 2.
  • Spoon combined sugar and miracle whip over salad.
  • Step 3.
  • Refrigerate until chilled. Toss just before serving.

Enjoy and Bon Appétit!
