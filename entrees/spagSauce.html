Mia's Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1
    lb Italian sausage
  • 1
    28-oz can roma tomatoes
  • 1
    15-oz can tomato paste
  • 1
    green pepper, diced
  • 1
    red pepper, diced
  • 1
    orange pepper, diced
  • 1
    onion, diced
  • 0
    Italian seasoning

Preparation

  • Step 1.
  • Crumble and fry sausage.
  • Step 2.
  • Saute peppers and onions in oil.
  • Step 3.
  • Add tomatoes and paste to sausage and vegetables.
  • Step 4.
  • Season to taste using salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, basil, oregano, thyme, garlic, or simply use Italian seasoning.

Enjoy and Bon Appétit!
